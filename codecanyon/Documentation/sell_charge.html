Sell Charge

There is many company who had multipule outlet cut a charge on sell form the outlet for various purpose. Like Central AC, Security, Fire fighting System, and many more

Create sell Charge
Note

Only admin can access this setting

  1. Click on Charges form the left sidebar
  2. Click on new Sell Charge
  3. Enter the purpose of sell charge
  4. Enter the % of sell charge
  5. Click to save save charge
Update sell Charge
Note

Only admin can access this setting

  1. Click on Charges form the left sidebar
  2. Click on Outlet Charges
  3. Click to edit a sell charge
Delete sell Charge
Note

Only admin can access this setting

  1. Click on Charges form the left sidebar
  2. Click on Outlet Charges
  3. Click to trash icon to delete a sell charge
