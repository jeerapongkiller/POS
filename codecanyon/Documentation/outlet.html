Outlet Management

Create an outlet
NOTE :

Only admin can create an outlet

    Login to your admin account

  1. Click on Outlet form the lift sidebar
  2. Click on new outlet
  3. Click on to add outlet logo. [Not mandatory]
  4. Enter your outlet name
  5. Select user type Outlet owner or modarator
  6. Outlet Location [min 10]
  7. Outley Monthly rent
  8. Additional charges / sell charges your can diselect charge by click on charge.
  9. Click to change tax information. otherwise it will takes the default tax information that given by admin during installation
  10. Click to save outlet
Update an outlet
NOTE :

Only admin can update an outlet

    Login to your admin account

  1. Click on Outlet form the lift sidebar
  2. Click on outlets
  3. Click on to edit outlet information .
    It will open outlet edit page and you will see the same thing as new outlet page to update outlet information
Delete an outlet
NOTE :

Only admin can delete an outlet
Once you delete an outlet you cannot able to recover it again

    Login to your admin account

  1. Click on Outlet form the lift sidebar
  2. Click on outlets
  3. Click on to deleye outlet
    Once you delete an outlet you will not able to recover it again
