Point Of Sale

    Login to your outlet owner / sells man account

  1. Click on sell from the left sidebar
  2. Click on New Sell to open Point of sale page
Note
POS Operation
  1. Select customer , leave if you dont want to add customer
  2. Create Customer, it will open a modal there you can cerate a customer
  3. Add Item/ Product by scaning product sky code
  4. Cancle this order
  5. Remove selected item / product form the list
  6. Make Discount
  7. Cancle this order
  8. Hold an order (Due order)
  9. Make payment
  10. Search product / item via name or sku
  11. Categorise item by click theses button
  12. View hold orders
  13. View direct customer order
  14. Item / Product. you need to click on them to add product / item to order list
Note
Hold Order
  1. Click to open hold orders
  2. Search order by ref number
  3. Click to delete order
  4. Click to print order
  5. Click to take payment
Customer Order
  1. Click to open customer orders
  2. Search order by customer name
  3. Click to delete order
  4. Click to print order
  5. Click to take payment
