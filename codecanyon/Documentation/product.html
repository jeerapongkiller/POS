Product / Item

Create a product / item
NOTE :

Only Outlet owner can able to create a product / item

    Login to your outet owner / manager account

  1. Click on product form the left sidebar
  2. Click on new product
  3. Click to place a product thumbnail
  4. Enter your product name
  5. Enter your product sku
  6. Enter your product price
  7. Select product categroy How to add product category
  8. Click to save this product
Update Product
NOTE :

Only Outlet owner can able to update a product / item

    Login to your outlet owner / manager account

  1. Click on product form the left sidebar
  2. Click on all product
  3. Clcik to edit product.
    You will see the same thing as new product with selected product details so your can change product info
Delete Product
NOTE :

Only Outlet owner can able to delete a product / item

    Login to your outlet owner / manager account

  1. Click on product form the left sidebar
  2. Click on all product
  3. Click to delete selected product
